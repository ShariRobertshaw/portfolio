Hypa apps

WEB DESIGN, UX/UI DESIGN, BRANDING

Hypa develops innovative apps for the BigCommerce platform, aiming to empower merchants with tools to enhance their online stores—especially those who may not have the budget for a custom-built solution. I crafted the brand identity, using geometric shapes to symbolise building blocks, inspired after I designed the logo which reflects the brand’s core values. For the website, I drew inspiration from space imagery to convey a sense of limitless potential, while ensuring the overall design felt sleek, premium, and tech-forward.

Hypa website design

UX DESIGN DESIGN SYSTEM RESPONSIVE WEB DESIGN

Role:

Digital Designer

Problem:

With the new brand identity in place, Hypa needed a clean, intuitive website to establish its credibility and showcase its app offerings to eCommerce merchants.

Requirements:

  • Translate the brand identity into an engaging web experience
  • Present app offerings clearly and guide users through content
  • Build trust with small to mid-sized merchants through design clarity and professional tone

Process:

Starting from the newly developed visual identity, I designed the website to reflect Hypa’s modern and innovative character. I incorporated abstract, space-themed graphics throughout the site to create visual flow and evoke a sense of exploration. Large, confident typography was used to guide users through the content and emphasize clarity at every level. The layouts were crafted for intuitive navigation, making it easy for users to explore available apps and engage with clear calls to action. I also paid close attention to responsiveness and structural consistency, ensuring the experience remained seamless across all devices. I created a design system for the site as well, to ensure a smooth handover to the developers and establish consistency for any additional designs.

Solution:

The final website clearly communicates Hypa’s product offerings and brand story. It paired striking visuals with a logical structure, helping merchants engage confidently with the brand. Combined with the identity work, the website positioned Hypa as a serious, innovative force in the BigCommerce space.
