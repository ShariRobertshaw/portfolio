Services and about me

What I can do for you

Fractional product designer

Digital design for agencies and studios

I design and build websites with CMS for small businesses. From initial concept to launch, I create custom Webflow sites that are beautiful, functional, and easy for you to update. Perfect for businesses that need a professional online presence without the complexity of custom development.

Career journey

I’m a designer based in Bath, UK, as well as a mother and a wife, and I genuinely love what I do. My career has taken me through a range of creative environments, from corporate and e-commerce to teaching, B2B marketing, and most recently a startup. Each step has shaped my approach and strengthened my ability to collaborate confidently, and lead impactful digital projects.

I have done a mix of digital design and product design over the last 5 years. I designed e-commerce websites, web extensions, dashboards, and web applications for startup and agency clients. I have gained experience in UX by conducting user research through video interviews, creating customer journey maps, developing user personas and scenarios, and designing user flows.

As part of a small product team, I collaborated closely with another designer to enhance the company's mobile apps. From mapping user flows to refining key interactions, I helped design features that balanced user needs with business goals. Through research, testing, and iteration, we shaped UI/UX updates that supported the overall product strategy. I also designed and developed several marketing websites using Webflow.

I contributed to and eventually led large-scale digital design projects for major brands. My responsibilities spanned the entire creative process, from pitching innovative ideas to collaborating with UX and development teams. I played a key role in establishing the creative vision for projects, either executing the designs myself or art-directing their implementation. Additionally, I spearheaded the development and implementation of a shared, flexible design system, which streamlined our processes and significantly improved the efficiency and consistency of our websites.

I designed e-commerce websites, created wireframes and advised on UX decisions, built prototypes, presented work our clients, and worked closely with the developers at Meanbee who then built the sites.

I worked in State Farm's in-house Creative Services department as a Graphic Designer. I conceptualized and designed for large internal and external events, and also worked on a marketing and advertising team that established advertising campaigns which ran nationwide. During my time there I art directed photoshoots and was lead designer on an internal publication, conceptualised creative for large initiatives, and worked on the branding team when the company established a new logo and brand.
