Gener8 Labs

WEB DESIGN, UX/UI DESIGN, RESPONSIVE BUILD IN WEBFLOW

Gener8 Labs is a B2B company that specializes in collecting and selling proprietary data sets. The data, gathered through the Gener8 app and browser extension, is anonymized and then used in an intuitive tool that empowers non-technical clients to make data-driven decisions. I was responsible for designing the website and then bringing it to life using Webflow, ensuring a seamless user experience that clearly communicates the value of their data solutions.

Web design and build in Webflow

UX DESIGN WEBFLOW BUILD WEB DESIGN B2B DESIGN CONTENT STRATEGY

Problem:

Gener8 Labs needed a website to communicate its B2B data capabilities to potential partners and clients. At the same time, the Labs brand was in its infancy and lacked a clear visual identity.

Process:

I started with wireframes of the content and layouts. The content came from our B2B sales team, and I worked with them to make sure the site felt easy to understand, but still kept the right tone for B2B businesses.


I then partnered closely with Gener8's Creative Director to establish the overall aesthetic, balancing trustworthiness with innovation. We explored typography, color schemes, and layout systems that aligned with the brand's emerging identity. Once the design direction was decided, I designed the pages in Figma then built the website end-to-end in Webflow.

Requirements:

  • Design a visually engaging website to position Gener8 Labs as a credible B2B partner
  • Support brand-building with a strong, cohesive visual identity
  • Develop the site in Webflow with clean structure and responsive behavior

Solution:

Delivered a polished, responsive website that showcased Gener8 Labs' capabilities while establishing a clear and consistent visual identity. The site was built entirely in Webflow, allowing for flexible updates and rapid iteration. The final product successfully positioned Labs as a serious player in the B2B data space and became a foundation for future brand development.
